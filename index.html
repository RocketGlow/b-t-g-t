My Experiences

I go places, then I write about it.

If I can't go physically, I go there in my mind.

Going somewhere mentally and not physically makes it difficult to take the kickass photos that I love to take while on vacation or even long weekend getaways and then of course post the shots on facebook, instagram, tweet them or even snapchat them to old friends, new acquantances... basically anyone I know in the hopes of making them a bit envious.

So when this is the case, I do the only logical thing to do and that is hunt for Creative Commons images on Flickr, SmugMug, et al. I usually find something decent to write about as if I was actually there.
